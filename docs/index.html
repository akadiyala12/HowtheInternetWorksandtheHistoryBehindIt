What is the Internet and Why Should You Care About it?

The internet touches everything around us. It went from being numbers in a computer to a way of life. It has the power to do tremendous good in the right hands and equal harm in the wrong hands.

The internet has woven its way into every facet of our lifestyles. Weather it be planning a birthday or ordering food or even talking to friends and family all over the world. The internet has brought those of us that are far from family physically much closer.

Knowing where and how the internet came to be could give you valuable insight on how it works for us today as well as where it could be going in the future.

When it comes to the internet, you, as a person have a lot on the line. The more you understand it, the better equipped you will be to not only use it but protect yourself from those on the interent that may cause harm to you.

This website will cover just a few topics about the internet and its creation. Use it to maybe spark an interest in something you wouldn't have thought about twice.
